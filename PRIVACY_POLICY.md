# Privacy Policy for TextFlow

**Last Updated:** February 5, 2026

## We Don't Collect Your Data

**TextFlow does not collect, store, or transmit any of your data to us.** Period.

- ❌ No analytics
- ❌ No tracking  
- ❌ No external servers
- ❌ No data sent to the developer

## Where Your Data Lives

All your snippets and settings are stored **on your device** using Chrome's built-in storage.

### Optional: Chrome Sync
If you enable "Sync Across Devices" in settings, Chrome may sync your data across your logged-in browsers. This uses **Google's encrypted sync** — not our servers. We never see this data.

| Sync Setting | Where Data Lives | Who Can See It |
|--------------|------------------|----------------|
| **OFF** (Local Only) | Only this device | Only you |
| **ON** (Chrome Sync) | Your Google account | Only you + Google |

**You control this.** Sync is optional and can be disabled anytime in Settings → Backup & Sync.

## Why We Need Permissions

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| `storage` | Save snippets on your device |
| `activeTab` | Insert text into the page you're on |
| `scripting` | Detect your shortcuts and expand them |
| `clipboardRead/Write` | Support copy/paste features |
| `<all_urls>` | Work on any website |
